At ChromeBird Inc., we handle your data with the precision and respect expected of an intelligence lab. This policy outlines our protocols.

01. Core Commitments

  • Your Data Control: When you provide us with access to your codebase for analysis, this data is processed solely to provide our services.
  • Immediate Deletion: Upon request, we will immediately delete your codebase data and any derived analytics from our active systems.
  • No Model Training: Your proprietary code will *never* be used to train our general AI models or any third-party AI models. Your IP remains yours.

02. Information We Collect

  • Direct Input: Code repositories, project data, and queries you submit.
  • Metadata: Standard traffic logs, IP addresses, and usage patterns to optimize system performance.

03. Usage Protocols

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Provide and optimize the ChromeBird platform.
  • Fulfill contracts and service requests.
  • Conduct internal research (excluding your proprietary code for training).

04. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact the lab directory: info@chbird.ai
